## About the Novel
*Sundered Fates* is a dark fantasy visual novel that follows a young protagonist on his journey to rescue his sister, who was kidnapped by the demon race years ago.

After being saved and trained by a mysterious figure, he finally sets out toward the land where she is believed to be, determined to bring her home regardless of what stands in his way.
What begins as a personal quest soon grows into something much greater, as each step forward reveals new truths, dangers, strange dreams, and new paths to follow.
Through branching choices and different story paths, the player will shape the protagonist’s journey, his relationships, and the fate of those around him.

But sometimes, fate does not move forward as cleanly as it should, and you will come to know it.
